Our Services

We offer a variety of unarmed security services designed to fit your needs:

  • Foot Patrols: Visible presence and regular patrols on foot to deter crime and monitor premises.
  • Vehicle Patrols: Mobile security patrols to cover larger properties or multiple locations efficiently.
  • Event Security: On-site management and monitoring for events, including crowd control and entry checks.
  • Beer Garden Security: Specialized security tailored for beer gardens and outdoor venues, ensuring safe and controlled environments.
  • Traffic Control: Managing traffic flow during events or on-site incidents to maintain order and safety.
  • Overnight Watch: After-hours surveillance and property checks to protect against vandalism and unauthorized access.

All services are performed by a licensed, experienced security professional focused on professionalism and discretion.
